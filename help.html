General FAQ's (Frequently Asked Questions)

  1. 1. What kind of health topics do you cover?

    2. We focus on practical health advice, including nutrition, fitness, mental wellness, and lifestyle tips to help you live a healthier life.

  2. 2. Who writes the articles on this website?

    3. Our content is created and reviewed by health professionals, wellness coaches, and experienced writers who research reliable sources.

  3. 3. How can I trust the health advice on this site?

    4. We ensure that all information is backed by reputable sources such as medical studies, expert opinions, and government health organizations.

  4. 4. Can I contribute my own health tips or personal experiences?

    5. Yes! You can submit your blog by clicking the "Add Your Blog" button at the bottom of the blog page.

  5. 5. How can I stay updated with new health tips?

    6. You can follow us on Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram, or sign up for our newsletter to get the latest articles directly in your inbox.

  6. How do I get in contact with the GQL personnel?

    7. Scroll to the bottom of any page and look for Contact Us. This should bring you to a page for personnel information.

Site Help

  1. 1. How do I read full blog posts?

    2. Click the “Read More” button on any blog preview to view the full article.

  2. 2. How do I comment on a blog post?

    3. Scroll down to the comments section, select the blog post you want to comment on, type your message, and click “Post Comment.”

  3. 3. How do I share a blog post with my friends?

    4. At the bottom of each blog post, you’ll find Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and an “Other” button that lets you share via Email, WhatsApp, Telegram, and more.

  4. 4. I want to submit my own blog post. How do I do that?

    5. Click the "Add Your Blog" button on the main blog page. This will take you to a form where you can submit your article for review.

  5. 5. What if I need help or have feedback?

    6. If you have any questions, suggestions, or need technical assistance, visit our Contact Us page, and we’ll be happy to help!

  6. How do I connect a Google account to this website?

    7. Sign in with you Google account email and password, and you will be redirected to your email fo a veification code. Once you answer the code you will be signed in as if you were on youtube.
