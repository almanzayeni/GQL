Easy Exercises

Staying active can boost mobility, reduce joint pain, and improve heart and brain health. These low-impact exercises are ideal for maintaining strength, balance, and flexibility without overexerting the body.

  1. Walking: A brisk 20-30 minute walk daily improves cardiovascular health and supports joint mobility with minimal strain.
  2. Chair Yoga: Gentle seated stretches help improve flexibility, posture, and circulation; great for those with limited mobility.
  3. Water Aerobics: Exercising in water reduces stress on joints while building endurance, flexibility, and muscle tone.
  4. Bodyweight Exercises: Moves like wall push-ups or sit-to-stands from a chair help maintain muscle strength and independence.
  5. Tai Chi: This slow, meditative movement improves balance, reduces fall risk, and lowers stress levels.
  6. Stretching: Daily gentle stretching keeps muscles limber, prevents stiffness, and improves range of motion.
